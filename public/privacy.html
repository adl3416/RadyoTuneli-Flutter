Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

✔ Zero personal data collected

1. Overview

Radyo Tüneli does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All app data is stored locally on your device only.

Thank you for using Radyo Tüneli. Your privacy matters to us. This app has been designed from the ground up to respect your privacy — no analytics, no tracking, no data sharing.

2. Data Stored Locally

The app stores the following data exclusively on your device using Android SharedPreferences:

  • Favorite radio stations (station ID list)
  • Recently played stations
  • App settings (theme preference, auto-play)

This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server.

3. Analytics & Tracking

This app does not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools. No Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, or similar services are integrated.

4. Internet Usage

The app uses the internet solely for:

  • Streaming live radio audio from third-party radio stations
  • Fetching the radio station list (via radio-browser.info public API)
  • Loading station logo images

No personal identifiers are sent with these requests.

5. App Permissions

  • INTERNET — Required for streaming radio
  • WAKE_LOCK — Keeps audio playing in the background
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — Background audio playback
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Playback controls in notification

6. Third-Party Services

Station list data is provided by radio-browser.info, a community-driven public API. Please refer to their privacy policy for their data practices.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You can delete all locally stored data at any time via Settings → Delete All Data in the app.
